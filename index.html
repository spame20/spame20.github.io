Helen Duque is a London-based senior editor and creative producer. She led post-production for Vevo Originals UK from 2020 to 2025 — running the full pipeline from ingest to final master, from official live films to European localisation.

Before that: theatrical trailers and TV campaigns at Ignition Creative (Elvis & Nixon, The Divergent Series, DOOH for Wonder Woman and Deadpool 2), and freelance editorial for Universal Pictures, Apple Music, Disney+, Paramount and CBS — lately including AI-led projects cut with Runway, Flow and Topaz.
